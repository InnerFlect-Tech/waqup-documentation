Domains to Reference (High-Level)

Neuroplasticity and Repetition

Repeated cognitive and emotional patterns strengthen corresponding neural pathways, supporting habit formation and behavioral reinforcement over time.

Attentional States and Relaxation

Calm, focused attentional states are associated with reduced cognitive noise and increased openness to internalized reflection and suggestion.

Self-Referential Processing (Own Voice)

Hearing one's own voice has been shown to increase emotional engagement, perceived relevance, and memory encoding compared to third-party narration.

Suggestion and Priming (Non-Hypnotic)

waQup does not induce hypnotic or altered states. It leverages well-documented priming and suggestion mechanisms within fully conscious, voluntary practices.

Required Framing

  • Non-clinical: waQup does not diagnose, treat, or replace professional mental health care
  • Non-therapeutic: Operates strictly within a self-guided wellness framework
  • Wellness-oriented: Focuses on personal development and identity reinforcement
  • Conscious, voluntary practices: All practices are fully conscious and user-initiated
