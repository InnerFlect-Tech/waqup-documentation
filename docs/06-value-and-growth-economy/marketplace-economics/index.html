Marketplace Economics

Creator economics, pack pricing, revenue distribution, and marketplace value exchange mechanisms.

Overview

The waQup marketplace enables creators (facilitators, coaches, therapists) to publish curated collections of rituals, meditations, and affirmations. The marketplace creates a value exchange between creators and users, supporting the growth of the waQup ecosystem.

Core Principle: Creators are compensated fairly for their expertise and curation. Users access high-quality, curated content that complements their own ritual creation.

Creator Packs

Pack Types

Single Ritual

  • Content: One curated ritual
  • Typical Price: €3-5
  • Use Case: High-value standalone rituals

Ritual Collection

  • Content: 5-10 rituals on a specific theme
  • Typical Price: €15-30
  • Use Case: Themed collections (e.g., "Morning Energy", "Sleep Rituals")

Complete Program

  • Content: 20+ rituals, structured program
  • Typical Price: €50-100
  • Use Case: Comprehensive transformation programs

Pricing Mechanisms

Direct Payment

Users can purchase packs directly with credit card (Stripe integration):

  • Immediate access: Pack available immediately after purchase
  • No credits required: Direct payment option
  • Creator receives payment: Revenue distributed according to revenue share model

Credit Conversion

Users can use credits to purchase packs:

  • Credit value: 1 credit = €3 value
  • Flexibility: Users can supplement with direct payment if needed
  • Creator receives payment: Credits converted to payment at €3/credit

Hybrid Payment

Users can mix credits and direct payment:

  • Example: Pack costs €25, user has 5 credits (€15 value), pays €10 directly
  • Flexibility: Maximizes user convenience

Revenue Distribution

Revenue Share Model

Standard Split: 70% creator / 30% platform

Pack Price Creator Receives Platform Receives
€5 €3.50 €1.50
€25 €17.50 €7.50
€50 €35.00 €15.00
€100 €70.00 €30.00

Payment Processing

  • Stripe fees: Deducted before revenue split (typically 2.9% + €0.30 per transaction)
  • Creator payout: Monthly payouts to creators
  • Minimum payout: €50 minimum before payout (to reduce transaction costs)

Credit Purchases

When users purchase packs with credits:

  • Credit value: Credits valued at €3 each for marketplace purchases
  • Creator payment: Creator receives 70% of credit value (€2.10 per credit)
  • Platform: Platform receives 30% of credit value (€0.90 per credit)

Creator Requirements

Quality Standards

  • Content quality: High-quality, well-structured rituals
  • Audio quality: Professional audio production
  • Description: Clear, accurate pack descriptions
  • Metadata: Proper tags, categories, and metadata

Publishing Process

  1. Creator application: Creators apply to publish in marketplace
  2. Quality review: Platform reviews first pack for quality
  3. Approval: Approved creators can publish additional packs
  4. Ongoing quality: Platform monitors pack quality and user feedback

Marketplace Discovery

Discovery Mechanisms

  • Search: Full-text search by keywords, themes, creator
  • Categories: Browse by category (e.g., "Sleep", "Energy", "Anxiety")
  • Featured: Curated featured packs
  • Creator profiles: Browse packs by creator
  • Recommendations: Personalized recommendations based on user's created rituals

Pack Preview

  • Description: Detailed pack description
  • Sample ritual: One ritual available for preview
  • Creator bio: Creator background and expertise
  • User reviews: User ratings and reviews

Relationship to User Credits

Key Distinction: Marketplace packs are separate from user credit system:

Aspect User Credits Marketplace Packs
Purpose Create own rituals Access curated creator content
Payment Credit packs (€9-60) Direct payment or credits
Content User-created rituals Creator-curated packs
Ownership User owns created content User licenses pack content

Principle: Credits enable creation. Marketplace enables discovery of curated content. They complement each other.

  • Creator tiers: Different revenue shares for different creator tiers
  • Bundles: Creator bundles or platform bundles
  • Gifting: Gift packs to other users

Summary

The marketplace creates a value exchange ecosystem that:

  • Supports creators - Fair compensation for expertise and curation
  • Enhances user experience - Access to high-quality curated content
  • Scales the platform - More content, more value, more users
  • Maintains quality - Quality standards ensure high-value content

Core Principle: Creators are compensated fairly. Users access quality content. Platform scales sustainably.
